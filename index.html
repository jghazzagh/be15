How to play

  • You are the new hire. Bizarre Bites runs on chaotic communication. Your job: catch the sentence errors before they cause a meltdown.
  • Read the case. Buzz Wordsmith and CEO Max Chaos set up each problem. Click the dialogue box to move forward.
  • Answer the challenge. Some are multiple choice. Some ask you to drag words into the right place. Some ask you to sort good sentences from broken ones.
  • Guard your reputation. Wrong answers cost you. Run out and the case gets tossed, but you can retry.
  • Earn your badge. Clear a case, get promoted. Clear all five and you run the building.
